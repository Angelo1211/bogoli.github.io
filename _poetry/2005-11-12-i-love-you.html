I love you’s aren’t said with words,
They are shown to the receiver through action,
The actions show them that you care,
Those are the laws of attraction.

I love you’s don’t mean a thing,
When they are naked without deeds,
With nothing else to go with them,
That’s not what anyone needs.

You’re my special person,
And since you’re the best, too,
I wrote you this poem,
To say that “I love you”.
