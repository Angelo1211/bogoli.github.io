Only orange makes me feel
As if I were a water wheel
Turning as life goes on and on
Orange makes me want to dance
as if ants were crawling in my pants
Dancing the night away
Orange is honey toast
with flavor fit to boast
Sweet, sweet honey
Tropical birds flying high
flying high in the clear sky
Orange bold birds
Orange burns inside of me
Burning like a fire free.
A single flame, burning bright.
