A portal to a magical world,
In the pages of a book.
The gift of letters and words awaits,
If you would just look…

Knights, queens, and dragons,
Fairies, and pixies, and leprechauns,
Things not even imaginable,
Waiting for an open door, a chance to escape,

So many things lie undiscovered,
So many places we could go,
Catch them before they’re gone,
Think of everything you could know…
