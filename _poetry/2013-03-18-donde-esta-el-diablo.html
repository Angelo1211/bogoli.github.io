--- layout: post title: 'Donde esta el diablo? ' date: '2013-03-18T07:20:00-04:00' tags: - poem - poetry tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/54967863711/donde-esta-el-diablo ---

I am in hell
yet I am content
how can this be?
Did I rid myself
of the devil inside me?
or did this just coincide
with a slide
and the divide
of my right mind
and the devil I’ve become.
