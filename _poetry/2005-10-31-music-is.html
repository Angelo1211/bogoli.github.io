A passage to your own world
A way to communicate
Pleasurable sounds
Jazz, funk, rock, skaa, rock, pop, classical
Something to do
Something to be done
Something to create
Something to weave
Something to become
Megahertzes
Vibrations and waves
Karma, whether good or bad

Feelings

Oohs and Ahhs
