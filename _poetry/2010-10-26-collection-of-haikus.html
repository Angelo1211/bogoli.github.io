Hot and heavy fall
Traitors from my eyes betray
liquid pearls of wet.

Pain wells in my heart
Frustration burning within
Time stops for no man.

Lost and scared she runs,
Bewildered by the knowledge,
Stealthily eludes.

Trembling heart she breathes
Breeze blowing calm on her brow
She presses onward.
