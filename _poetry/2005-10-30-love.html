Making your heart beat,
Chocking you in your throat,
The smile of a stranger,
Melts your insides,
Is something wrong with me,
That the littlest attention
makes me giddy for a day?
Or is it called love,
That you fall head over heels into,
It is a sea, pulling you in,
Never letting go,
Until one day you’re alone,
Sad and lonely, friendless…
So much for love.
