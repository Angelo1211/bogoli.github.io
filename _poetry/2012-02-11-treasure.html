In the nothingness of this world,
Do you think there might be treasure?
If someone would look,
Just take a measure,
Try to find that hidden treasure…

Burried [sic] on an island,
Or in a stack of books,
It might take years of searching,
Or just a few looks,
Under the oldest books…
