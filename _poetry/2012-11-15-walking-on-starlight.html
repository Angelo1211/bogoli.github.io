--- layout: post title: walking on starlight date: '2012-11-15T20:46:00-05:00' tags: - poetry tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/54966242596/walking-on-starlight ---

I can’t keep a smile
from taking a seat
on my lips
this starless night
I’m feeling starlight all over me
can’t keep my head
from nodding to the beat
can’t stop my hips from swaying
or my mind from straying
(to you)
moments pass like a movie montage
can’t wait for the next reel
and it comes so fast
Good to sea you too
So this is what it feels like to be happy.
I could do with more revelations like this.
