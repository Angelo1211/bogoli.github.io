Campus

Belongs to Human drift
Intermediate model for THE BIG SAIL
painted steel
West North East West
Certainly Please do NOT
Identify yourself.
man has devised Dormitory Stairwells
Seek / Leave Alternate Route
To avoid the perils of the tide
Mass roof access. Please be patient thank you
for emergency dial 100
and the bartender replies,
There is nothing to writing. all you do

* a poem digitally constructed of pictures, signs, and posters found around MIT
