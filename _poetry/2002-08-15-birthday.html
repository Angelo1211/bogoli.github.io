It’s my BIRTHDAY!
The cake is ready
Today is Thursday
the day of excitement
All is perfect
Switch that tape
Just press eject
Oh, that’s great
Dim the lights,
Just a tad
Get out the kites
for our guests
Let’s the cut cake
Oh, so pretty
Let’s hope it’s not
too baked
Hooray!
what a specialy
wonderful
stressful day!

(08.15.2002)
