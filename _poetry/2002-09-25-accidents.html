Accidents can happen
Though they are unpleasant
They can happen to a king
or a common peasant
Sometimes they are fatal
Sometimes they are not
Sometimes it can be replaced
Sometimes it can be bought
Sometimes it can be hurtful,
Physical I mean
As big as an elephant
as small as a bean
Who will care?
we are all here together
to bear what we can bear
