I need
A giant key
To unlock the door
Of knowledge
A giant key,
Gilded,
And gold.
Paired with a rusty,
Broken
Lock.
The lock only works
Halfway.
The door only opens
Halfway,
And knowledge is the light,
That seeps from the
Small crack
My giant key unlocked.
My giant key,
Gilded
Golden
Ancient
Skeleton
Key
To the door
Of knowledge.
