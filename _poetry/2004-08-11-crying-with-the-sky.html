--- layout: post title: Crying with the Sky date: '2004-08-11T17:13:00-04:00' tags: - '2004' tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/938710602/crying-with-the-sky ---

Raindrops fall like tears,
Falling from the sky
For the events of today
Had made me want to cry.

As I stare up above
Staring at the sky
The sky matches my feelings,
My feelings of cry.

As I cry so hard,
Gazing at the sky,
I don’t cry alone,
for I’m crying with the sky.
