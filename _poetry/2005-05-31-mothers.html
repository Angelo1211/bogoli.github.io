Mothers smile,
and mothers care,
A mother’s love,
is always there,
Through the good times
and the bad,
When you’re happy,
mad, or sad,
A mother is a guide,
A teacher and a friend,
A crying shoulder,
Until the end
No matter what,
A mother loves you,
with her whole heart,
She doesn’t care what you do.
She loves you.
