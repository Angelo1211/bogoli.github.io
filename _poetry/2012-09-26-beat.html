beats pulse
like I’ve never heard them
and math comes through my fingers
but it starts in my brain
like I haven’t felt
in an awful long time
what was I doing to myself?
how is it that things now make sense?
Or do they?
is this what normal is?
whatever it is
I’m hoping that it never stops
because I never want to
stop
like that
again.
