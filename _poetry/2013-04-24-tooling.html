If you are tooling in Athena webs,
In the great blue yonder,
When vitality of learning ebbs,
here, this question, ponder:
Could I join you in your fight,
and tool away into the night?

We were tooling in Athena net
but our Santiago has a sleep debt
now, don’t you, however, fret,
a Kevin’s company you can still get.
