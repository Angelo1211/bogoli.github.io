--- layout: post title: radio silence date: '2013-01-30T20:16:00-05:00' tags: [] tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/54968361732/radio-silence ---

it’s not like I can feel you here
your warmth, your touch, your eyes on me
you’re far away
connected through wires and invisible waves
and yet when you leave
I feel just as empty
as if you walked out of the room.
