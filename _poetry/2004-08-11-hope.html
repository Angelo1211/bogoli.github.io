When we are alone and sad
It guides us like it always had.
in the dark it’s a glimmer
In the dark it’s a shimmer
Making you a little better, glad.

In the night, in the dark
It’s singing like a lark,
Pulling you through,
Helping you renew,
Even if it’s just a spark.

What would we do without hope,
I believe we could not cope,
Pulling us,
Drowning us,
Without hope, we could not
Hope.
