--- layout: post title: load-bearing beams date: '2012-10-28T11:15:00-04:00' tags: - poem - poetry tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/54953877371/load-bearing-beams ---

I’m breaking down walls
busting them open
going places I’ve never gone before
but in my rage against the man
and in my zest for life,
I tore down walls
load-bearing beams that support me
and now, painstakingly,
I must build them up again.
where is my rage and my zest now?
