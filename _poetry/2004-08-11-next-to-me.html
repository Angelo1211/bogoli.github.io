Sitting quietly
Next to me
is noisyness
and darkness
the brothers who roam free,

They make things frightening
Like thunder and lightning
Shadows in the dark
howling wolves in the park
Then it’s gone, the sky lightning.

Peacefulness comes in,
time and time again,
Like deep sleep,
or quietness knee deep
Like it’s never been.
