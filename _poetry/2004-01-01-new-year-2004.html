As we welcome the new year
Which is coming now and here
We will sit and sigh,
Maybe even cry,
about the things that have past
in the year here last
But new things are coming our way
Have you not nothing to say?
We will start the year with a clean slate
Not the way we are, in our present state
We will make resolutions,
To problems, create solutions
Be better next year!
So come on out and cheer,
5, 4, 3, 2, 1!
Le’s jump into the New Year’s fun!
