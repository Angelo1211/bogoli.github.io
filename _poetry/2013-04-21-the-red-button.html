Persimmon polymer
pesters parcels of persona
piercing passive pastels
pushing poison pervasively,
progressively paring at perseverance
parsing points of peculiarity,
plucking at prudent, preplanned purpose.
peaceful, penultimate period
poised, perched,
paused, preceding
petitie paradigm permutation
provoking the pointed push.
perilously plummeting plunger
perfectly perceived
pop.
