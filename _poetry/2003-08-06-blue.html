Blue. A mix of sadness and loyalty
The sky, oh, so blue and true
The filling of yummy blueberry pie
The water in the salty ocean blue

A color, of royalty, midnight sky
Your friend’s glittery, sparckaly glue
My trusty pair of jeans
My mom’s favorite leather sofa blue
