An albatross swims
while an alligator walks
A bumblebee buzzes
while a big parrot squawks
A little dog barks
A cow emits a moo
and a cat gives a screech
at a sparrow who flew
A bear rambles along
A wolf gives howls
A monkey leaps from branch to branch
While a black leopard yowls.
