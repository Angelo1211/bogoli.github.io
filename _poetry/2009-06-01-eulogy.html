1
Oh little kitty
so dear to my heart
why have you gone?
so horribly
so painfully
You deserved to die
no pain,
Not the way you did.
The last things you saw,
Some stranger standing over you
In pain
Crushed by rubber
Coughing blood.
I should have been there
to hold you
to stroke your soft orange fur,
to let you know I love you.
Never will you walk beside me,
Tangle in my legs
purring like a lawnmower,
Oh how I loved you.
I’m so sorry that you’ve gone.
I love you.
I love you.
My crazy orange cat.
My garfield.
