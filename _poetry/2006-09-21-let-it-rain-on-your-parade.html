--- layout: post title: Let it Rain on Your Parade date: '2006-09-21T12:00:00-04:00' tags: - '2006' tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/48193117998/let-it-rain-on-your-parade ---

Sing in the rain,
Dance in the street,
Go overboard,
& set sail today.

Don’t give a damn,
If it rains on my parade,
Life doesn’t end,
When the floats get wet.
The candy still flies,
Wet though it is,
Straight to the greedy
Children’s hands

Jump into life,
Just do it, go ahead.
Nothing to it.
Step into space,
Dive headfirst,
Don’t worry about the
Safety net,
It’s not there,
It won’t ever be.

Jump,
Head over heels,
For the one,
You love,
Even if,
They break your heart.
Let it rain.
On your parade.
