Christmas time has come
A-jingling and a-ringing
Winter snows have come,
A-caroling and a-singing,
Warm cookie time has come,
A-sneaking and a-eating,
Drummer boys are coming,
a-stamping and a-beating,
Santa Clause has come,
a-hauling and a-lugging,
