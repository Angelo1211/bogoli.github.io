--- layout: post title: The World and Life date: '2002-08-16T14:42:00-04:00' tags: - '2002' tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/913683446/the-world-and-life ---

The world is cruel,
unfair, just like it’s brother Life
Why can’t everyone be nice
an all day long, play the fife?
Why, tell me?
Why can’t it be?
But the world has
it’s beauty, too,
Lots of flowers.
So let’s not boo
and let the world
be true.
