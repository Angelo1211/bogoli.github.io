Weary bones
Tired muscles
Aching, stretching
sore, exhausted.
Energy seeps from every pore.
Blood, like sap,
gurgles through my veins
every beat of my heart
Almost more effort than I can make.
Blissful relief
as darkness slides over the field
of vision… Release.
unconsciousness.
to let go… into the ocean of dreams.
