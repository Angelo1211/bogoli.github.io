Your heart,
is empty,
your mind,
is empty,
no feelings,
no thoughts,
no emotion,
no nothing.
no pain clenches at your heart, no joy makes it swell
I’m numb,
Just a rock
in a sea of nothingness.
the waves wash over me,
yet nothing leaves a mark.
I don’t feel anything.
I’m empty.
