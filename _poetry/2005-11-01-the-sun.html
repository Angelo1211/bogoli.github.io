I couldn’t imagine,
what it might be like,
to never see the sun again
to live under the cover of darkness
to never see light
sweet, bright, beautiful,
rays of light.
To come out with the moon,
When it conquers the realm,
of darkness and shadows,
with only a single beam,
of soft, silky moonlight
to guide your life…
