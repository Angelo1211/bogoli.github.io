--- layout: post title: Lament on the back of a Calculus Test date: '2010-11-20T18:06:00-05:00' tags: - poem - free verse - poetry - spilled ink tumblr_url: http://poetry.liabogoev.com/post/54946785302/lament-on-the-back-of-a-calculus-test ---

Sitting
Waiting
in vain hope
that knowledge
has seeped into my mind
and will now drip
through graphite
and magically appear
in formulas
on the paper.
Every few minutes,
chalk numbers are replaced,
by counter parts smaller and smaller
and vain hope dims
into non-existence.
