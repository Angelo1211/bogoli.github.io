sensual silhouettes
I look more beautiful
as a shadow on the wall
than I ever looked in person
faceless.
  .
commonplace cotton
I feel more beautiful 
as a sensation on a sheet
than I ever felt dancing on ice
graceful.
  .
flickering flames
I sound more beautiful
as soft breaths in scented air
than I ever sounded in person
silent.
