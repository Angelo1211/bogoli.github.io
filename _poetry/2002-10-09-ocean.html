Dark and mysterious
Mean and universal
in the shallows it’s calm
So it’s very diversal,
Because it can writh [sic]
and twist in the middle
It is very unpredictable
Like solving a new riddle
Being in the ocean
It feels like a bubbling
witches brew or potion
But it can be pleasant
